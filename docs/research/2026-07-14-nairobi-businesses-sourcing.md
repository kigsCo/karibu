## Sources & Method

**Method:** Candidates were found via Google/web search (best-of guides, directories) and then each business was verified against a primary or reputable secondary source — preferring the business's own website, then Tripadvisor / EatOut / Fresha / KenyaBuzz / recognised travel blogs. Contact and location fields were copied from those sources; nothing was invented. Where a business's own site could not be reached or a fact could not be confirmed, the field was left blank or a directory/Tripadvisor listing was cited instead.

**Main directories / guides used:**
- Fresha (salons/spas): https://www.fresha.com/lp/en/bt/hair-salons/in/ke-nairobi
- Tripadvisor Nairobi restaurants & salons: https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurants-g294207-Nairobi.html
- EatOut Kenya (restaurant listings/booking): https://eatout.co.ke/
- nairobirestaurants.co.ke, KenyaBuzz directory, Go Places Kenya
- Travel guides: heleninwonderlust.co.uk, theexpatmummy.com, afar.com, angama.com, bestkenya.ke
- Transport: techweez licensed e-taxi list, jkiairport.com, lalacabs.co.ke car-hire roundup
- Business's own sites (primary): ashleyskenya.com, poshpalace.co.ke, rapunzelhairaffair.com, serenityspa.co.ke, blossomspa-ke.com, barberkingskenya.co.ke, thetalismanrestaurant.com, cultivakenya.com, tinroof.cafe, tamarind.co.ke, javahouseafrica.com, artcaffe.co.ke, legrenierapain.co.ke, kenatco.co.ke, marketcarhire.com, little.africa, faras.com, pollmans.com, bonfireadventures.com, gametrackersafaris.com, southerncrosssafaris.com

**Considered but dropped (and why):**
- **Mama Rocks Gourmet Burgers** — well-known Westlands/Kilimani burger brand, but at least one source (toasterding.com) reports it "packed up shop" in Oct 2024. Dropped pending confirmation it is still operating, per the currently-operating rule.
- **The Loft Beauty Spa (IG @the_loft_beautyspa)** — surfaced in searches but the reachable profile appeared to be US-based (Florida), so it could not be confirmed as a Nairobi business. Dropped to avoid a wrong listing.
- **Generic JKIA "airport taxi" SEO sites** (e.g. several nairobi-*cabs.com / airport-transfer landing pages) — many are thin lead-gen pages of uncertain operator identity; excluded in favour of named companies with a verifiable corporate presence (Kenatco, Market Car Hire, Europcar, Avis). The team may still add a vetted independent transfer operator later.
- **Seven Seafood & Grill / Fogo Gaucho / CJ's** — named in initial search prompts but I could not pull a clean primary source with current contact/location in this pass; left out rather than listed on weak evidence. Good candidates for a follow-up.

**Caveats for the team before seeding:**
1. Several **phone numbers came from directories/Tripadvisor rather than the business's own site** (About Thyme, Habesha, Avis Kenya, Pam Nail Polish) — re-confirm before publishing.
2. **Chains** (Ashleys, Java House, Artcaffe, Serenity Spa, Blossom Spa, Le Grenier à Pain) have multiple branches; decide whether Karibu seeds one HQ listing or per-branch listings.
3. **No coordinates** were captured reliably — geocode addresses at seed time rather than trusting any lat/long.
4. **Salons came in at 10** (target ~12): the category is dominated by Westlands/Kilimani, so consider adding a verified Karen/Lavington hair salon and a second dedicated spa for spread. Restaurants (12) and rides (10) are within target.
5. About Thyme's own site (about-thyme.com) returned a TLS certificate mismatch during research; verify the domain is still correctly served before linking it.
