# Business Owner Preview Guide

The full partner-facing preview guide — for business owners invited to test Karibu before public launch — is distributed as `karibu-business-guide.pdf` by the Kigs Apex team.

This markdown version is a placeholder so the docs folder stays discoverable from GitHub.

## What's in the full guide

1. What is Karibu, really
2. How to open the preview on your phone
3. The five-minute walkthrough
4. Trying it as your business
5. How listings and rankings actually work
6. The three plans, side by side
7. Questions business owners have already asked
8. How to send feedback

The guide is designed for Nairobi SME owners and can be opened cleanly on any phone via WhatsApp. Version 1.0 is dated April 2026.

## Distribution

The guide is sent directly to invited partners alongside their preview URL. It's not publicly distributed.

If you're a business owner reading this repository and you haven't received the guide, contact partners@karibu.co.ke.
